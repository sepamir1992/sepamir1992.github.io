About

I am a PhD candidate in Electrical and Computer Engineering at the University of Victoria. My research focuses on intelligent networking, wireless communications, computer networks, AI/ML-based network optimization, and adaptive communication systems.

My work combines networking, machine learning, reinforcement learning, and optimization to design efficient, adaptive, and resilient communication systems.

Research Interests

Wireless Networks

Adaptive wireless systems, multi-connectivity, and next-generation communication networks.

Multipath Communication

Scheduling, congestion control, and performance optimization for multipath transport protocols.

AI/ML for Networks

Learning-based resource allocation, network intelligence, and data-driven optimization.

Federated Learning

Privacy-preserving distributed learning for edge and communication systems.
