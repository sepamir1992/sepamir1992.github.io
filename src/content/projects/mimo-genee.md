## Goal

Research communities need reproducible benchmarking, but dataset distribution and challenge management are typically handled through ad-hoc, fragmented workflows.

## My Solution

Designed and built MIMO GenEE, a research web platform handling dataset hosting and distribution, challenge registration, and submission management — making benchmarking reproducible and accessible for the research community.

## Impact

**Completed and delivered to Université de Montréal**, the project stakeholder.
