## Overview

Satellite links — especially LEO constellations — bring rapidly changing topology, handovers, and highly variable path characteristics. This ongoing project designs a **satellite gateway** that helps with routing and makes transmission smoother by using **MPQUIC at the transport layer**, letting traffic exploit multiple paths across the satellite-terrestrial network simultaneously.

## Key directions

- Gateway-assisted routing for satellite network traffic
- MPQUIC-based multipath transport across heterogeneous satellite/terrestrial paths
- Smoother handovers and transmission continuity under dynamic topology

This work connects to my collaborations on LEO satellite beam scheduling (IEEE PACRIM 2024, IEEE TCCN 2026).
