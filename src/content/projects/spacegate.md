## Goal

Dynamic LEO-style satellite constellations bring rapidly changing topology, handover events, and highly variable link conditions. Static routing cannot keep mission traffic flowing smoothly across such Hybrid Space Networks.

## My Solution

Building **SpaceGate**, an intelligent gateway prototype validated in an emulated LEO-style environment, combining real-time telemetry, adaptive path scoring and selection, MPQUIC-like multipath traffic steering (switching, splitting, failover, and priority duplication), and prediction-assisted control.

## Impact

Ongoing **6-month TRL-4 project**, building toward a validated laboratory demonstrator showing measurable improvement over static routing for satellite-terrestrial traffic.
