## Goal

## My Solution

Led a multi-team effort (frontend, backend, database, algorithms) to build scheduling software balancing student preferences, faculty availability, and classroom resources.

## Impact

- Reduced scheduling conflicts by **70%**
- Compressed scheduling time from **3 weeks to 2 days**
